01The Note

Find out who you are
and do it on purpose.

That's the line you live by and it shows. Not in a loud way, in the way you carry yourself: the tilt of the cap, the easy confidence in a park full of strangers, the calm in a kurta at golden hour. Some people dress for the day. You dress like every day might end up on someone's camera roll, and somehow it never feels forced.

A year older today. Same sharp eye, same quiet swagger, hopefully an even better playlist. Here's to the next lap around the sun may it be exactly as on-brand as this one.
